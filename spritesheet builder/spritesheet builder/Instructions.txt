-max sprite sheet size is 256x256
-program will only accept .png files
-program will sort by height to save space

To mack a sprite sheet, click the "add sprites" button. a selection window will pop up,
Multiselect all files you want and hit "open".
A preview of the sprite sheet will pop up, if it is not to your liking
you can re-select the files.
When you are happy with the preview hit "save sprite sheet"
and a new window to pop up to save the sprite sheet to where you want.
Hit save and you're done!
